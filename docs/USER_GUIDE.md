## Mobile Experience

### Installation as App (PWA)

#### On iPhone/iPad

1. Open Safari and visit StreamFlix
2. Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"**
5. App icon appears on your home screen

#### On Android

1. Open Chrome and visit StreamFlix
2. Tap the **Menu** (three dots)
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install App"**
4. Tap **"Install"** or **"Add"**
5. App icon appears on your home screen

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Is StreamFlix free?**
A: Yes! This is a demo application showcasing OTT platform capabilities.

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: No account required for this demo version.

**Q: Does it work offline?**
A: The app interface works offline (PWA), but videos require internet connection.

**Q: What video format is supported?**
A: MP4 (H.264) format is supported in all modern browsers.

### Content Questions

**Q: Why do all movies play the same video?**
A: This is a demo using a sample video (Big Buck Bunny). Production version would have unique content for each title.

**Q: Can I download movies?**
A: Not in this demo version. Future versions may include download functionality.

**Q: How often is content updated?**
A: This is a static demo. Production version would have regular content updates.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Why isn't the video playing?**
A: Check your internet connection and ensure your browser supports HTML5 video.

**Q: The app is slow. What can I do?**
A: 
- Clear browser cache
- Close other tabs
- Check internet speed
- Try a different browser

**Q: Images aren't loading?**
A: This could be a network issue. Refresh the page or check your connection.

**Q: Can I use it on my smart TV?**
A: Yes, if your TV has a web browser. Navigate to the URL using your TV's browser.

### Video Player Questions

**Q: How do I make the video fullscreen?**
A: Click the fullscreen button in the video controls, or double-click the video.

**Q: Can I adjust video quality?**
A: Quality adjusts automatically based on your connection (adaptive streaming would be in production).

**Q: Why does the video pause sometimes?**
A: This could be due to slow internet. The video buffers to prevent interruptions.

### Mobile Questions

**Q: Does it work on tablets?**
A: Yes! Fully responsive on tablets (iPad, Android tablets).

**Q: Can I cast to my TV?**
A: If your browser supports Chromecast, yes. Look for the cast icon in the video player.

**Q: Why can't I install the app?**
A: Ensure you're using a supported browser (Safari for iOS, Chrome for Android).
